About ELECOURSE

Every semester, ELECOURSE members collaborate to complete a full engineering project from hardware implementation to algorithm development and validation. Our projects emphasize practical engineering experience, research-oriented thinking, and interdisciplinary teamwork. ELECOURSE 회원들은 매 학기 하드웨어 구현부터 알고리즘 개발 및 검증에 이르기까지 완전한 엔지니어링 프로젝트를 공동으로 수행합니다. 저희 프로젝트는 실질적인 엔지니어링 경험, 연구 중심적 사고, 그리고 학제 간 팀워크를 강조합니다.

Team Structure

Hardware Team

Sensor integration, embedded systems, STM32 firmware development, PCB and acquisition systems.

Signal Processing Team

ECG/PPG filtering, feature extraction, PAT/PTT computation, signal quality assessment.

Machine Learning Team

Dataset construction, regression models, hyperparameter optimization, performance evaluation.

Validation Team

Experimental protocol design, clinical interpretation, statistical validation, result analysis.

Current Project

End-to-End Cuffless Blood Pressure Estimation Pipeline Design


An integrated biomedical engineering project involving ECG/PPG acquisition, signal processing, feature extraction, machine learning-based blood pressure estimation, and experimental validation.


  • 20 Participants
  • 84 Measurements
  • STM32 + AD8232 + MAX30102
  • PAT/PTT-based Estimation
  • SVR, Random Forest, Hybrid LASSO-XGBoost
