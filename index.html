About ELECOURSE

ELECOURSE is a student-led engineering research group that conducts semester-long collaborative projects across biomedical engineering, signal processing, embedded systems, machine learning, and intelligent healthcare technologies.


Every semester, members form interdisciplinary teams to design, implement, and validate a complete engineering system, from hardware development to algorithm design and experimental evaluation.

Research Areas

Biomedical Signal Processing

ECG, PPG, EEG, biosignal filtering, feature extraction and physiological analysis.

Machine Learning

Regression, classification, healthcare AI, and predictive modeling.

Embedded Systems

STM32, sensor integration, real-time acquisition systems, wearable devices.

Digital Healthcare

Wearable monitoring, cuffless blood pressure estimation, and personalized medicine.

2026 Spring Project

End-to-End Cuffless Blood Pressure Estimation Pipeline

This project developed a complete cuffless blood pressure estimation system based on synchronized ECG and PPG signals. The framework includes:

  • Hardware Design (STM32 + AD8232 + MAX30102)
  • Signal Acquisition
  • Signal Processing Pipeline
  • PAT/PTT Feature Extraction
  • Machine Learning Prediction
  • Validation and Performance Evaluation
